Traveling through North America, Dongfang Zhao's work is inspired by the sensibility of light and shadow. Through retrospective drawings, these moments of mundane are distilled into traces of passing gaze. By employing spatial and temporal separation, along with process-driven materials, Zhao continuously folds and challenges the boundaries between creator and viewer, the present and memory, reality and imagery. The resulting paintings open up a stage for the audience to share and explore.
