Currently on View at A/W Space
"Autumn Winter Space (A/W Space) was established in 2021 in Nanjing, China. It is dedicated to promoting contemporary art and street culture, actively discovering emerging artists who reflect contemporary spirit, and continually nurturing the market for established artists. As a gallery that integrates art with living spaces, A/W Space offers an alternative to the traditional white-box gallery model.

"We aim to explore diverse experimental exhibition formats while fostering the collision and fusion of Eastern and Western ideologies. In addition to regularly curating exhibitions, the gallery frequently organizes cross-disciplinary events such as salons, film screenings, music appreciation sessions, and culinary experiences. By viewing space as a medium, A/W Space seeks to create a lifestyle platform centered on art."
